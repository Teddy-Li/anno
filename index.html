Instructions

Short Summary

Paraphrase a given sequence of questions into integrated natural question, then rate the question sequence.

Detailed Instructions

  • You will be given a sequence of questions, these questions are created by machines.
  • Combined, each sequence of questions forms a query to a certain piece of information.
  • An example of desired answer is also presented in 'Answer Sample'.
  • An example of similar question is also provided for your reference.
  • Your job is to read the question sequence and answer sample carefully to understand what it asks for, then rewrite the question in your words.
  • You may either ask the question in one sentence or a few sentences as you wish.
  • After you rewrite the question, indicate whether this is grammatically valid, whether there are redundant parts and whether it's something that people may actually ask.
  • Before you start off, please read the following positive and negative examples carefully. ^_^
